About the Club

The ODS Christian Club is a club open to all students at Old Donation School. The club provides a respectful and welcoming space where students can grow in faith, character, and community through prayer and fellowship.

Our goal is to encourage kindness, leadership, and positive values through the Christian faith. Participation is always optional, however we encourage you to come to as many meetings as possible.

Mission Statement

The mission of the ODS Christian Club is to encourage students to live out their faith through prayer, meaningful discussion, and acts of service. The club seeks to support personal growth, build positive relationships, and inspire students to lead with integrity, compassion, and respect within the school community.

Statement of Faith (Nicene Creed)

We believe in one God, God the Father, the Pantocrator, Creator of heaven and earth, and of all things seen and unseen.

We believe in one Lord, Jesus Christ, the only-begotten Son of God, begotten of the Father before all ages. Light of Light, true God of true God; begotten, not created; of one essence with the Father; by whom all things were made. Who for us men and for our salvation came down from heaven, was incarnate of the Holy Spirit and of the Virgin Mary, and became man.

And He was crucified for us under Pontius Pilate, suffered and was buried, and on the third day He rose from the dead according to the Scriptures. Ascended into the heavens; He sits at the right hand of His Father, and He is coming again in His glory to judge the living and the dead, whose kingdom shall have no end.

Yes, we believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the Giver of Life, who proceeds from the Father, who with the Father and the Son, is worshipped and glorified, who spoke by the prophets.

And in one holy, catholic (universal), and apostolic church. We confess one baptism, for the remission of sins. We look for the ressurection of the dead, and the life of the age to come. Amen.

Constitution / Proposal

Purpose

The purpose of the ODS Christian Club is to provide a space where students can gather to grow in their Christian faith and character. The club seeks to encourage prayer, fellowship, service, and thoughtful discussion while promoting respect, kindness, and positive values within the school community.

Activities

The ODS Christian Club may include a variety of age-appropriate and school-approved activities, such as:

  • Student-led prayer and reflection
  • Bible reading and group discussion
  • Fellowship and encouragement among members
  • Discussions on character, leadership, and living out faith
  • Sharing the Gospel in a positive and non-coercive manner
